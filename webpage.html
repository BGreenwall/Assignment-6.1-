Best Video Games

Game 1:

Stardew Valley is easily ranks #1 in best games. It has adventure, role playing, fighting, farming, and so much more. The immersive storyline with memorable characters makes the well-rounded game deserves first place.

Game 2: Undertale is very 'underrated' ;D for it has multiple endings, insane character development, and 4th wall easter eggs. This game deserves the love it still gets after years of being released

Game 3: Insaniquarium is the type of cozy and mindless game that allows you to take your mind of things. Similar to plants vs. zombies, this game has levels and upgrades involved, which motivated the player to get further. Thus ranking 3rd place

Game 4: Wizard101 is an oldie but a goodie. It has a captivating story line, unqiue charcaters, and one of the best games to become the strongest player in the game. The dual shadow magic your wizard receives enchanced the entire gameplay, ranking it 4th

Game 5: Finally.. among us is a classic game to have some arguments and ruin a friendship or two. Like UNO, this game is excited to play with friends and really bring each other together. The revengful tactic also allows people to have a good time, thus ranking 5th place.
